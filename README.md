

# Flapjack

*preheat oven to gas mark 4*

- 8oz butter/clover
- 12 level tbsp treacle
- 1.5oz caster sugar 
- 16oz oats


Melt treacle and butter together in a large saucepan  
When liquid put sugar in an stir until dissolved.
Turn gas off  
Add oats, mix in pan
Gently press into baking pan

Bake 12-15 min
